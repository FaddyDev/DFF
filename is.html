Information Systems

| | |

Information systems (IS) help in transforming businesses today. The systems have helped businesses achieve operational excellence, improve decision making, competitive advantage, making work easier, reduce production costs while improving profits, customer and supplier satisfaction among other benefits.

Our software products include

  • Websites - We develop websites to help you communicate to a multitude of your customers all over the world. Our websites are search engine optimized to enable quick access.
  • Blog sites - We help bloggers pour out their mind to their readers all over in an easy way. We develop sites that will attract and communicate to your readers.
  • Management information systems - these are meant to help business to keep track of factors affecting them internally to ensure productivity and profitablity. Some of them include:
    • Human Resource Management System - help to keep track of staff numbers, attendance, leave, performance...ensuring accountability.
    • Inventory Management System - help to keep track of items, equipments, stock to ensure their availability and good condition always.
    • School Management System - can be library system, financial management system, students welfare management system in separate or all of them combined into a single system that allows the performance of the above activities simultaneously and synchronously.
    • Hotel Management System - Stores prices of meals and keeps track of the money earned everyday then compares with the expenditure to show profitability. Can also keep manage the human resource in the hotel. For bigger hotels the system can be made to enable online booking.
    • Livestock Management System - To help farmers manage their livestock and keep track of their feeding, treatment and production. Also manages farm human resource and finances.
    • Cooperative Society Management System (SaCCoS) - manages human resource, members' registration and contribution among other operations of the specific saccos.

      • The list is long, it goes on and on depending on the needs of our customers. We develop applications that satisfy the customer requirements fully.
  • Decision Support Systems - these are expert systems that help managers in making decisions. They make decisions that are in line with the firm's goals and criteria and save mmanagers the time, work and stress of arriving at crucial decisions.
